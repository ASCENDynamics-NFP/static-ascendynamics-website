Empowering Worker-Owned Cooperatives to Succeed

ASCENDynamics provides technical, networking, and other resources to startups and nonprofits. Join ASCENDynamics and help grow a new community!

Who We Are

ASCENDynamics is a non-for-profit organization that incubates worker-owned cooperatives. Our mission is to help entrepreneurs create successful startups by providing technical, marketing, development, funding, and networking resources.

Our Services

ASCENDynamics offers a variety of services to support worker-owned cooperatives and nonprofits. From technical assistance to marketing support, we help our clients succeed.

For Startups

ASCENDynamics is here to help startups succeed. We provide a variety of resources to help entrepreneurs create worker-owned cooperatives, including technical, marketing, development, funding, and networking resources.

For Nonprofits

ASCENDynamics is a platform for nonprofit organizations to collaborate, increase their outreach, and improve their impact. We offer a variety of resources to help nonprofits achieve their goals.

Our Think Tank

ASCENDynamics is committed to creating a utopian society. Our think tank is a space for creative and innovative ideas to flourish, and we are always seeking new solutions to the challenges facing our world.

Contact Us

We'd love to hear from you! Contact us today to learn more about how ASCENDynamics can help you.

Connnecting Non-Governmental Organizations

A non-governmental organization (NGO) is a non-profit group that functions independently of any government. NGOs, sometimes called civil societies, are organized on community, national and international levels to serve a social or political goal such as humanitarian causes or the environment.

Our Goal at ASCENDynamics is to help build a platform for NGOs to collaborate and in turn be more effective providing a wider reach.

Cooperative Startup Workspace

A cooperative is an autonomous association of persons united voluntarily to meet their common economic, social, and cultural needs and aspirations through a jointly-owned and democratically-controlled enterprise.

At ASCENDynamics we realize our business and entrepreneurial goals by helping startups form cooperative business structures.
